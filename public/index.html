Core Invention

Three pillars of link integrity

📸

Cryptographic Snapshot

At creation, we fetch your destination page, normalize the content, and compute a SHA-256 hash. This is your tamper-evident seal.

🔍

Real-Time Verification

Every single click re-fetches the destination and recomputes the hash. Compared instantly to the baseline — in milliseconds.

📊

Modification History

Every detected change is logged with a timestamp. See exactly how many times and when a page was modified since you shared it.

Technical Process

How it works

01

You submit a URL

Our system fetches the destination page using a headless crawler. JavaScript-rendered content is captured for accuracy.

02

Normalization Pipeline

Volatile elements are stripped: ads, timestamps, navigation, cookie banners, sidebars, social counters. Only meaningful content remains.

03

SHA-256 Baseline Hash

The normalized content is cryptographically hashed using SHA-256. This 64-character fingerprint is stored with your short link.

04

Every click triggers re-verification

When someone clicks your short link, we re-fetch and re-hash the destination page in real time. Hash comparison takes milliseconds.

05

Integrity Indicator delivered

Before redirecting, the user sees a clear trust signal: ✓ Unchanged since shared, or ⚠ Modified N times. Then they proceed to the destination.
